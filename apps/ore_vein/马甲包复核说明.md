## 1. 马甲包功能

**Ore Vein**（显示名）定位为「野外 / 矿物桌边参考」的离线英文工具：`FieldNote` 与 shaft 偏好经 `shared_preferences` 持久化，不依赖对局或业务服务端。

启动后先进入 **`Qkzmt0` 门闸**（与原 `app_common` boot 等价：远程首包 + Rule A 联网/离线逻辑在生成文件内）。首包为 **JSON 数组**，仅用**首个对象**驱动路由；字段名为随机前缀 `qkzmt*`（见第 2 节）。`qkzmtPlaf` 为 `"1"`/`"2"`/`"3"` 时分别进入应用内 WebView 两支或外开浏览器；缺失或无效则进入本地五栏壳。应用内 UI **不出现** `remote_url`、`MockAPI`、endpoint 等字面文案。

**除「Shaft controls（设置）」外五个可见主区块（底栏直达）：**

| 名称 | 进入方式 |
|------|----------|
| Atlas | 底栏第 1 项 |
| Mohs | 底栏第 2 项 |
| Streak | 底栏第 3 项 |
| Kit | 底栏第 4 项 |
| Vault | 底栏第 5 项（工具栏可加新 Field Note） |

**Shaft controls**：顶栏右侧「Shaft controls」图标进入（竖分栏图标）；含主题与套件类设置、`FieldDeskPersistence`（Vault）、剪贴板与清空类操作。

**详情/编辑**：从 Vault 行进入 `FieldNoteEditorPage`（`noteId == null` 为新建）。

**Promotional Text**

Atlas, Mohs, streak, kit, vault—Ore Vein keeps mineral cues and trench notes beside you offline. Shaft tunes the desk; nothing nags about remote setup.

**Description**

Ore Vein is an English-only field desk for people who sketch geology on the couch or at a bench. Atlas gives quick-reading cards without pretending to replace a handbook. Mohs Bench walks the hardness ladder with plain-language blurbs tied to recognizable specimens. Streak Board layers powder-line colors side by side for classroom-style comparisons. Field Kit exposes inspector toggles you can trust on a commute—density presets, mohs overlays, and other shafts you flip in Shaft Settings. Vault is your offline specimen shelf: jot a trench-side hunch, anchor it to a mineral key, revisit it anytime, export a manifest when you share notes with collaborators. Shaft Controls houses the quieter preferences—theme washes, typography heft, toolkit switches, Vault list breathing room, clipboard exports, and safe wipes—all stored locally. Every surface stays offline-first, ATT only appears when attribution partners need it, and the interface never quizzes you about configuration URLs.
